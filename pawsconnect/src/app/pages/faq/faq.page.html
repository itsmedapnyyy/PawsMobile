FAQs

What is PawsConnect?

PawsConnect is a web-based portal and mobile application designed to simplify the pet adoption process. It connects potential adopters with pets from Nativity's Stray Animals Rescue Shelter and provides resources for post-adoption care.

How do I create an account?

Follow these simple steps to create your account:
1. Visit the PawsConnect homepage.
2. Click on the "Sign Up" button.
3. Fill in the required information and create your password.
4. Check your email for a confirmation link to activate your account.

How do I log in?

To log in, simply:
1. Go to the PawsConnect homepage.
2. Click on the "Sign In" button.
3. Enter your email and password.
4. Click "Log In" to access your account.

How does the pet adoption process work?

The adoption process involves:
1. Submission of documents
2. Review and notification
3. Schedule an ocular visit
4. Final approval and adoption.

Can I choose the pet I want to adopt?

The shelter owner recommends a pet based on your preferences and the pet's needs to ensure a perfect match.

Where can I find pet care resources?

Visit the "Pet Care" section of the PawsConnect platform for articles and tips on pet care, training, nutrition, and health advice.

What should I do if I forget my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page, click "Forgot Password?", and follow the instructions to reset your password.

How can I update my account information?

Log in, go to "Account Settings", and update your information as needed.

Can I donate to the shelter?

Yes! Visit the "Donations" section for more details on how you can contribute to Nativity's Stray Animals Rescue Shelter.
