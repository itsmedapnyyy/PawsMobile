How to Help Stray Cats & Dogs?

Nativity's Stray Animals Rescue Shelter has two types of Donation:

A monetary donation allows you to directly support the care and well-being of rescued animals. By donating money, you contribute to the overall operation of the shelter.

An in-kind donation involves giving goods or services rather than money to the shelter. You can give your donation to the shelter by visiting them directly.
