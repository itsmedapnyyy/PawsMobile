About Us Nativity's Stray Animals Rescue Shelter is a non-profit organization located in Puerto Princesa City, Palawan. The shelter helps adopters find new families for stray cats and dogs.

Mission & Vision

Mission: Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome stray and rescued animals, ensuring their health through awareness, advocacy, and government animal welfare services.

Vision: Our vision is to create a humane and compassionate future for every rescued animal.
